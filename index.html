Who We Are

Epikboost is a software company that believes in building relationships.

Be it may for a business or for personal use, together we can develop epic applications and digital experiences that cater to your requirement. We do that by following 4 simple steps.

Define

We kick off the process by siting down with you and understanding your idea.

Design

Then we start researching and create initital UI/UX prototypes and revise changes with you.

Develop

After that we start developing the app and create an initial build for you to test.

Launch

Finally we conduct further tests, fix bugs and release the product to the public.

Our Expertise

We are constantly invested in researching and using latest and gratest technologies.

Everythng from designing, protoyping and full-stack development to hosting and promoting your product, we offer a wide range of services for you to choose from, all under one roof.

Web Development

We develop web apps using plain HTML, CSS, Javascipt or using front end frameworks like React or Vue. For the back end we use .NET, Node, Python, MySQL, MongoDB etc. depending on your requirements. We also develop wordpress web apps if you quickly need a website running.

Mobile Development

We are heavily invested in creating incredible mobile experiences for users. We develop native cross-platform mobile applications using React-native and Flutter for both iOS and Android. We use Firebase, Netlify, Azure and other hosting services to deploy your app.

Business Automation

Need a software for your business? An inventory management system for your shop? Automate business processes that would otherwise take time consuming labor? Look no further. We make cutting-edge cross-platform desktop applications using Electron and .NET.

Cloud Services

Gone are the days where you have to maintain expensive physical servers and lose sales when servers shut down. Migrate your servers to the cloud and pay as your business grows. We provice cloud services from Azure, AWS, Firebase as well as Heroku and Netlify.

UX/UI Design

UI of your applications determines weather the user wants to keep using it or just get bored and move on. One of our expertise is creating powerful user experinces that keeps the user constantly engaged. We use Adobe XD, Photoshop, Figma etc. to create cutting-edge prototypes.

Digital Marketing

You got your app ready, great now what? Promote your business on social media, improve your search ranking with SEO or PPC and create engaging graphics and content for your audience. We also specialize in e-commernce and payment gateway integration.
